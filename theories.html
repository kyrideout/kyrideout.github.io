Theory #1: Conspiracy

What if the disciples invented the whole thing as a conspiracy? They came to the tomb, got around the guards, stole the body and lied about it to the whole world.

Death by Crucifixion

In this theory Jesus died and stayed dead. No contradiction here.

Empty Tomb

The disciples stole the body, which explains the empty tomb.

Postmortem Appearances

In this theory the disciples lie about seeing Jesus. They know that they stole the body of Jesus and that he is actually still dead.

Birth of the Church

This theory, however, can’t account for the birth of the church. If we look into the lives of the disciples, we see that all but one of Jesus’ close friends were martyred for trying to spread the story that Jesus rose from the dead. If these men knew that they whole thing was a hoax, why would every single one of them be willing to give up their social status, family and friends, and even their lives, for a lie that gave them no personal benefit?
